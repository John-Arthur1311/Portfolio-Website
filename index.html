About Juan

I am an instructor and educator specializing in English education, literacy development, and curriculum design. My professional work centers on making learning meaningful by connecting academic content with cultural relevance and student experience.

I actively integrate popular culture — including anime, digital media, and fictional narratives — into teaching and research, using these mediums to foster critical thinking, collaboration, and deeper engagement in English and composition courses.

Education thrives when learning feels relevant, thoughtful, and human.
